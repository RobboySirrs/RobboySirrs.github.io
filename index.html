How to Calculate Safety Stock and Reorder Point (with Real-World Variability)

Reorder point (ROP) isn’t just “average sales × lead time.” That only works in a perfect world. If you’re serious about avoiding stockouts, you need to factor in **variability** — both in your lead time and your sales rate.

Here’s the formula I use in real businesses:
ROP = (Average daily sales × Average lead time) + Safety stock

To calculate safety stock properly:
Safety Stock = Z × √(Lead Time Variance × Daily Sales Variance)
Z is the service level factor (e.g., 1.65 for 95% confidence).
